Pulao

Pulao is a fragrant rice dish similar to biryani, but simpler. It's a flavorful combination of rice, meat (usually mutton or chicken), and aromatic spices.

Ingredients

  • Rice: 1 cup (Basmati is recommended)
  • Meat: 1/2 kg (mutton or chicken, cut into pieces)
  • Onions: 2 medium, sliced
  • Ginger-garlic paste: 1 tbsp
  • Green chilies: 2-3, slit
  • Bay leaf: 1
  • Cardamom: 2-3 green
  • Cinnamon: 1-inch stick
  • Cloves: 4-5
  • Star anise: 1
  • Yogurt: 1/2 cup (optional)
  • Ghee/Oil: 2 tbsp
  • Salt: to taste
  • Water: 2 cups (adjust as needed)
  • Fresh coriander: for garnish

Steps to make Pulao

  1. Marinate the meat with ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, salt, and yogurt (if using) for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat ghee or oil in a pot. Add bay leaf, cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, and star anise. Saut√© for a few seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add the marinated meat and onions. Cook until the meat is browned and the onions are softened.
  4. Wash the rice and add it to the pot. Stir well to coat the rice with the spices and meat juices.
  5. Add water, bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover the pot, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the water is absorbed.
  6. Turn off the heat and let the pulao rest for 5-10 minutes before serving.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander.
