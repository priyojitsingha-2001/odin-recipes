Ingredients

  • rice 2 cups
  • Meat 500 grams
  • Yogurt 1/2 cups
  • Onions 3 large
  • Tomatoes 2 medium
  • Ginger garlic paste 2 tablespoons
  • ghee or oil 4-5 tablespoons

Stpes to make Biryani

  1. Marinate the meat with yogurt, spices, and herbs.
  2. Parboil the rice with whole spices for flavor.
  3. Cook the meat or vegetables with spices, onions, and tomatoes.
  4. Layer the rice and cooked meat/vegetables in a pot with fried onions, mint, and coriander.
  5. Add saffron-soaked milk, seal the pot, and cook on low heat (dum) for 20-30 minutes.
